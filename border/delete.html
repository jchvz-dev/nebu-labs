How to Delete Your Account

This page provides step-by-step instructions for deleting your Border app account. If you delete your account, your personal information will be removed from our systems.

Steps to Delete Your Account

Follow these simple steps to permanently delete your Border account:

  1. Sign in to your account
    Open the Border app and sign in using your email/password or third-party authentication (Apple, Facebook, or Google).
  2. Open the Settings menu
    You can access Settings in two ways:
    • Tap the menu icon (three horizontal lines) at the top of the screen, OR
    • Swipe right from the left edge of the screen
  3. Tap "Delete Account"
    Scroll to the bottom of the Settings menu and tap the "Delete Account" button.
  4. Confirm deletion
    A confirmation prompt will appear. Read the information carefully, then confirm that you want to delete your account.

⚠️ Important Information

What happens when you delete your account:

  • Your account information (email, authentication data) will be permanently deleted
  • Your contributions (status updates, wait times) will be immediately anonymized and cannot be linked back to you
  • You will no longer be able to access your account or personal crossing history
  • This action cannot be undone

What is preserved:

  • Your anonymized contributions (status updates, wait times) remain in our system to maintain data integrity for wait time calculations
  • This data cannot be traced back to you or your account

Guest Users

If you are using the Border app as a guest (without creating an account), your contributions are already anonymous and are not linked to any personal information. No account deletion is necessary.

Alternative: Signing Out

If you don't want to permanently delete your account but simply want to sign out, follow the same steps to access Settings, but tap "Sign Out" instead of "Delete Account." You can sign back in at any time.

Data Deletion Requests

If you've already deleted your account through the app but want to ensure all your data has been removed, or if you're unable to access the app to delete your account, you can submit a data deletion request by email:

  1. Send an email to contact@nebu-labs.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days

California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to request deletion of your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). To exercise this right, follow the steps above or contact us at contact@nebu-labs.com with "California Privacy Rights Request" in the subject line.
