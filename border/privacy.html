2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address (may be proxied through authentication services like Apple Sign-In)

Device Information:

  • Device type and model

  • Mobile network information
  • App version
  • IP address

Usage Data:

  • Features you use within the App

4.2 Aggregated and Anonymized Data

We use user contributions (status updates, wait times, location data) to calculate and display wait time estimates:

  • Guest users: All contributions are anonymous by default and cannot be linked to any individual
  • Account users: Contributions are associated with your account for your personal history. If you delete your account, all your contributions are immediately anonymized and cannot be traced back to you
  • Aggregated wait time data displayed to all users is calculated from anonymized contributions and cannot identify individual users

This ensures your privacy while maintaining accurate wait time predictions for the community.

4.3 User Contributions

When you submit status updates or wait time information:

  • This data is associated with your account (if you have one) for your personal history
  • Guest users' contributions are anonymous and cannot be linked to any individual
  • All users can view aggregated wait time data, but cannot see individual user contributions

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Management

You can sign out of your account or delete your account through the App settings.

5.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time. Upon deletion:

  • Your account information will be deleted from our active systems
  • Your contributions (status updates, wait times) will be immediately anonymized and cannot be linked back to you
  • Anonymized contributions may remain in our systems to maintain data integrity for wait time calculations

5.3 Location Data

You can opt out of location tracking at any time through your device settings or App permissions. However:

  • Geofencing features will not function without location access

5.4 Data Export

In future releases, you will be able to view and export your personal border crossing history and contributions.

  • Categories of personal information collected
  • Sources of personal information
  • Business purposes for collection
  • Categories of third parties with whom we share information

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.

Right to Opt-Out:
You have the right to opt out of the "sale" of your personal information. We do not sell personal information in the traditional sense, but advertising through AdMob may be considered a "sale" under CCPA. You can opt out through your device's advertising settings.

Right to Non-Discrimination:
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

How to Exercise Your Rights:
To exercise your California privacy rights, contact us at contact@nebu-labs.com with "California Privacy Rights Request" in the subject line.
